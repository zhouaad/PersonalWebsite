Notable Projects:

  • Pong Soccer: Interactive Robot Game
    • Completed in Fall 2024.

    • Pong Soccer is an interactive robot game where players are split into two teams, yellow and blue, competing to score goals. The game's objective is to guide a robot equipped with a soccer bin to the opposing team's goal by throwing ping-pong balls into designated bins. Motion detection sensors attached to the bin detect ball proximity using Raspberry Pi, sending WebSocket messages to a ROS2 node to control the robot's movement when proximity exceeds a threshold. The robot also triggers sound and LED lights for audio and visual feedback. At the start of the game, the robot is positioned in the center of the field, and players stand near their goals with ping-pong balls. Teams throw balls into their bins to influence the robot's movement towards the opposing team's goal. The first team to move the robot to the opposing goal wins.
